Green Bean Potato Pesto Salad

Description

This recipe is a simple, delicious summer favourite using fresh homemade pesto, local green beans & soft, boiled potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Small Potatoes
  • Green Beans
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Pine Nuts
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 3 or 4 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 Lemon (optional)
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Start by boiling your potatoes & green beans in seperate pots
  2. Add Pine Nuts & Parmesan Cheese to a food processor. Pulse.
  3. Add Basil leaves to food processor. Pulse.
  4. Chop & add garlic cloves to food processor. Pulse.
  5. Add olive oil to your pesto mix, a little at a time, pulsing in between until you achieve your desired consistency.
  6. Add salt & pepper to your pesto to taste.
  7. If desired, add the juice of half a lemon to your pesto.
  8. Once your green beans & potatoes are done cooking, drain water and add both to serving bowl.
  9. Add your pesto sauce to potatoes and green beans and mix to cover vegetables.
  10. You're done! Serve warm, or store in the fridge for up to 3 days and enjoy cold.
